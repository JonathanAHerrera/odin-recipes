Arroz Recipe

Picture of Mexican Rice

Ingredients per cup of rice

  • 1 Small Tomato
  • 1/4 of an Onion
  • 1 Tablet of Consume de Pollo
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Wash Rice and let dry for 30 minutes
  2. Heat olive oil on medium low heat and toast rice until nutty smell appears
  3. Blend Tomato, Onion, Consume de Pollo, and Garlic
  4. Add the mixture to rice adding water to complete the ratio of 2 cups of water per cup of rice
  5. Bring rice to a boil
  6. Cover with air-tight top and lower heat to low
  7. Let sit for around 18 minutes or until the rice has absorbed all water
  8. Turn off heat and keep covered for an extra 10 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
