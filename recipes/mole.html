Mole

Picture of Mexican Dish Mole

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Abuelita
  • 2 Containers of Mole
  • Chicken Breast
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chile
  • Coke Can

Steps

  1. Boil Chicken with half an onion and two cloves of Garlic
  2. Once Chicken is boiled (20-30 minutes) lower Heat
  3. Add one container of mole, half a bottle of coke, one chocolate abuelita, and one chili to blender
  4. Add water from boiled chicken to blender until it is 1/2 - 3/4 full
  5. Blend until liquid consistency
  6. Add to low heat pan
  7. Repeat steps 3-5 and add to same low heat pan
  8. Add chicken to pan and cook sauce until it thickens
  9. You are done!
