Mexican Beans Recipe

Image of Mexican Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of beans
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • Half on Onion

Steps

  1. Place Beans, Garlic, and Onion into cooking pot
  2. Fill with water until beans are completely submereged
  3. Boil water and cover
  4. Cook for 6 to 8 hours re filling with water whenever beans are no longer submerged
  5. Enjoy!
